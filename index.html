Some common questions
were often asked

What is Fetch?

Fetch is a logistics and delivery startup that connects users to a pool of independent couriers or contracted delivery services for quick, affordable, and convenient delivery options in Nigeria.

What challenges does the logistics and delivery industry in Nigeria face?

The logistics and delivery industry in Nigeria is plagued by several challenges, including government policies, poor infrastructure, insecurity, multiple taxes, and unstable economic conditions.

What are the key application features of Fetch's platform?

Fetch's key application features include real-time tracking, multi-modal delivery, a rating system, a pooling system, in-app payments, and driver authentication.

How does Fetch ensure high-quality delivery service?

Fetch ensures high-quality delivery service through its rating system, where customers can rate their drivers and provide feedback on their delivery experience. This information is then used to continually improve the service, identify areas for improvement, and ensure that the drivers are providing the highest quality delivery experience possible.

How does Fetch make its delivery service more affordable for small businesses and individuals?

Fetch's pooling system is a cost-saving feature that allows it to combine multiple deliveries into one trip, reducing delivery costs and making its service more affordable for small businesses and individuals.

How does Fetch ensure the security of its platform and deliveries?

Fetch has implemented a robust driver authentication system that ensures only authorized and trusted drivers have access to its platform. It requires all drivers to undergo a thorough background check and identity verification process before they can join the platform. In addition, strict security protocols and measures have been implemented to ensure that only authorized drivers can access the platform and accept deliveries.
